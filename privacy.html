Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Celebalike ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Data: When you use Celebalike, you may take a selfie using your camera or select a photo from your photo library. This image is used solely to perform facial analysis and find your celebrity lookalike.

Facial Data: The app generates a temporary facial embedding (a mathematical representation of facial features) from your photo. This is used to compare against our celebrity database.

Subscription Data: If you subscribe, Apple handles all payment processing. We do not collect or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your photo and find celebrity matches
  • To display match results within the app
  • To generate shareable result cards

3. Data Processing and Storage

All facial analysis is performed entirely on your device. Your photos and facial data are never uploaded to any server. The facial embedding is generated locally using on-device AI models and is not stored permanently.

We do not:

  • Upload your photos to any server
  • Store your facial data after the session ends
  • Share your photos or facial data with any third party
  • Use your photos for any purpose other than finding celebrity matches

4. Celebrity Database

Our celebrity database contains publicly available information (names and profile photos) sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB). This data is stored locally on your device as part of the app. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

5. Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit: We use Apple's StoreKit framework to manage subscriptions. All payment processing is handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles your payment data.

6. Children's Privacy

Celebalike is intended for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at liamsapps.suport@gmail.com and we will take steps to address the issue.

7. Data Retention

Photos you take or select are held in temporary memory only during your active session. Once you close the results screen or exit the app, this data is discarded. We do not maintain any database of user photos or facial data.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All processing happens locally on your device.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or other jurisdictions with data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Request deletion of any data we may hold
  • Opt out of data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at liamsapps.suport@gmail.com.

9. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All facial processing occurs on your device using industry-standard AI models. No data is transmitted over the internet during the facial matching process.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
