2. How We Process Your Photos

2.1 On-Device Processing

When you take a selfie or select a photo, the following happens entirely on your device:

  1. Your photo is loaded into temporary memory
  2. An AI model (SCRFD) detects your face and identifies facial landmarks
  3. Your face is aligned to a standard position for comparison
  4. An AI model (ArcFace R50) generates a 512-dimensional mathematical embedding
  5. This embedding is compared against pre-computed celebrity embeddings stored locally
  6. The closest matches are displayed
  7. Your photo and embedding are discarded when you leave the results screen

2.2 No Server Communication

At no point during this process is any data sent to any server. The AI models run locally on your device's processor. The celebrity database is bundled within the app itself. No internet connection is required for facial matching.

2.3 Shared Results

If you choose to share your results, a share card image is generated locally on your device. The sharing is handled by iOS's native share sheet. We do not intercept, store, or have access to shared content. Where you share it (Messages, Instagram, etc.) is governed by those platforms' privacy policies.

3. Biometric Data

3.1 Definition

The facial embeddings generated by our AI models constitute biometric data under certain privacy laws (including the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act, Texas CUBI, and Washington state law).

3.2 Our Handling of Biometric Data

  • Collection: Facial embeddings are generated on-device only when you actively use the scan feature
  • Storage: Embeddings exist only in temporary device memory (RAM) and are never written to disk or persistent storage
  • Retention: Embeddings are automatically destroyed when you close the results screen, switch apps, or close the app
  • Sharing: Embeddings are never transmitted, shared, sold, or disclosed to any third party
  • Purpose: Embeddings are used solely for comparing your face against our celebrity database within the same session
  • Destruction: No manual deletion is necessary as data is automatically purged from memory

3.3 Consent

By using the camera or photo selection feature of the app, you consent to the temporary on-device processing of your facial data as described above. You may withdraw consent at any time by simply not using the scan feature or by deleting the app.

4. Celebrity Database

The app contains a pre-built database of celebrity names, thumbnail photos, and pre-computed facial embeddings. This data is:

  • Sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB), a publicly available database of entertainment professionals
  • Stored locally within the app bundle on your device
  • Not modifiable by users
  • Used solely for the purpose of facial comparison matching

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

  • Do not have access to your Apple ID or App Store account details
  • Only receive a binary subscription status (active or inactive) from Apple's StoreKit API
  • Store your subscription status check locally on your device

For information about how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 General Rights

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) do not apply in the conventional sense. However, you always have the right to:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Delete the app which removes all local data including the celebrity database and any cached preferences
  • Revoke camera permissions through iOS Settings at any time
  • Cancel your subscription through iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

9.2 European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to Access: We do not store your personal data on any server. All data exists temporarily on your device only.
  • Right to Rectification: Not applicable as we do not maintain records of your data.
  • Right to Erasure: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. No server-side data exists to delete.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can restrict processing by revoking camera/photo permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as no persistent personal data is stored.
  • Right to Object: You may stop using the app at any time.
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision Making: The app uses AI for facial matching for entertainment only. No decisions with legal or significant effects are made.

Legal Basis for Processing: Our legal basis for the temporary on-device processing of facial data is your explicit consent, which you provide by choosing to take a photo or select an image for matching.

Data Protection Officer: For GDPR inquiries, contact us at liamsapps.suport@gmail.com.
